About Yvette Harlow

Yvette Harlow is a local clothing brand that embodies the essence of freedom in every design. Our catalog consists of garments that serve as blank canvases for our customers to express their uniqueness and individuality.

Whether it's femme frilly dresses, boyish jerseys, or the shortest mini skirts, Yvette offers pieces that evoke a sense of gentleness, sensuality, and freshness. We believe fashion should be liberating - our designs celebrate all forms of self-expression without boundaries.

Each piece is thoughtfully crafted to be versatile, allowing you to style them in countless ways that reflect your personal aesthetic. From Rp 99.000 to Rp 139.000, we make expressive fashion accessible without compromising on quality or design integrity.
